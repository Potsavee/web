VALORANT Champions Tour 2025

is the fifth official tournament circuit by Riot Games. The circuit was announced on August 20th, 2024 with a YouTube video. More details on Championship Points and international tournament formats were shared on November 26th, 2024

Format

Pro System

  • Four international leagues, each in a different region, with 12 teams each.
  • 10 partner teams and 2 teams from Ascension (one from 2023 and one from 2024)
  • Leagues, the regions they cover and their bases are as follows:
    • Americas Leauge
      • Covers North and South America.
      • Based at the Riot Games Arena, Santa Monica, Los Angeles, California, United States
    • China Leauge
      • Covers Mainland China.
      • Based at the VCT CN Arena, Huangpu, Shanghai.
    • EMEA League
      • Covers Europe, the Middle East and North Africa.
      • Based at the Covers Europe, the Middle East and North Africa.
    • Pacific League
      • Covers Asia and Oceania outside of Mainland China and West Asia.
      • Based at the Sangam Colosseum, Mapo-gu, Seoul, South Korea.
